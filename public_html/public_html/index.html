Working Title not included: Disclaimer

Hi! Welcome to the demo version presentation of my game "Working Title not Included". I hope you enjoy the janky experience.

Just wanted to drop a quick disclaimer about some of the quirks of what you're about to witness.

The 'Play' buttons are a safety measure for playing audio files; Browsers have been largely discouraging autoplaying audio on websites, and on this one it's integral to the gameplay. If the audio does not play after 3 seconds, try pressing the button. When the music starts playing, the button should disappear. If it didn't, don't worry, it doesn't affect the gameplay.

Press L and R arrow keys to control character in game, use mouse to control pellet.
